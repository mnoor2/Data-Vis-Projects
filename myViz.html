World Population Density Map

The geomap visualizes the population density of each country based on its LogDensity. LogDensity is a calculated value that helps to mitigate the impact of extreme outliers in the original data, which would otherwise distort the color shading and make it difficult to identify trends.

As expected, countries like India, Pakistan, and China, which are home to a significant portion of the world's population, exhibit high population density. Interestingly, the US, despite having a large population, does not appear to have an equally high density.

One limitation of this visualization is the challenge of effectively representing outliers using color coding. To address this, the LogDensity transformation was employed to improve the color shading and enhance the visibility of trends.
